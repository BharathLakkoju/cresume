# Certifications & Courses

---

## Do Certifications Matter at MNCs?

**Short answer**: They matter less than projects and DSA, but they signal genuine learning investment and help at resume screening (especially for AI/ML roles and cloud roles).

| Tier | Value of certs |
|---|---|
| Tier-1 (Google/Amazon/Microsoft) | Low — projects and DSA matter far more |
| Tier-2 (Flipkart, Razorpay) | Low-medium — strong projects still win |
| Tier-3 (SAP, Oracle, Cisco) | Medium-high — enterprise certs carry weight |

## Priority Order (where to spend learning time)

1. DSA practice (LeetCode/NeetCode) — highest ROI for cracking interviews
2. Core role skills (depth in your stack)
3. System design (Grokking + DDIA)
4. Projects (build, deploy, document)
5. Certifications (nice to have, not make-or-break)
